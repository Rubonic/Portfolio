Recipe

This was a project I started for my MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node) full stack course final.This blog is a opportunity for individuals to post their favorite recipes to share with the world.
The individuals has a variety of nationalities to select from such as American, German, Mexican, Chinese, Indian, Thai and Desserts.
Customers have the ability to be only one that edit their own recipes no matter if it is to improve it or remove it for the site completely.

Technologies:
  • - MongoDB
  • - Express
  • - React JS
  • - Node JS
  • - Postman
